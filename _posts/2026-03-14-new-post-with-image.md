## Here is an example!

This is a sample post to demonstrate how image and categories will be displayed. When you assign an `image` in the front matter, it will show up on both the thumbnail grid and the full-page post. If you leave `image` out, the grid will fall back to a default picture.

You can also assign one or multiple `categories` to sort your grid by them.

Enjoy your new blog layout!
